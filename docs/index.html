Welcome to my page.

I am a computational neuroscientist focused on Bayesian methods for inferring large-scale brain network dynamics. I also work with deep learning and electrophysiological data from rats and humans.

Postdoctoral researcher at Institut de Neuroscience des Systemes (Inserm UMR 1106), Aix-Marseille University, France.

Research Focus

  • Bayesian inference

    Parameter estimation and probabilistic modeling for whole-brain simulators.

  • Network neuroscience

    Structure-function coupling, synchrony, and information transfer in networks.

  • Computational modeling

    Dynamic models of brain networks, neural synchrony, and excitability.

  • ML + neuro data

    Deep learning pipelines for electrophysiology in animal and human recordings.
